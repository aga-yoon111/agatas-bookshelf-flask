Mere Christianity

Readed on: 15/07/2025

Rating:

★ ★ ★ ★ ★

Review:

A book that explores the Christian faith in an accessible and profound way, ideal for Christians and non-Christians seeking to understand its foundations. The author, known for his ability to translate complex concepts into clear language, presents a comprehensive overview of the Christian faith, addressing topics such as the moral law, free will, conduct, and the nature of God.
